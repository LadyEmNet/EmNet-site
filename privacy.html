Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 October 2025

EmNet Community Management Ltd (“EmNet”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) operates this website to provide information about our services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information we collect

This website itself does not actively collect personal information. However, certain information is collected automatically by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) when you visit the site. This may include your IP address, browser type, and standard log data necessary to deliver the site securely.

If you choose to contact us by email or Telegram, we will receive the information you provide (such as your name, email address, Telegram handle, or message content).

How we use information

We use any information you provide solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry or providing the services you request. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Cookies

This site does not set cookies by default. If we introduce analytics or service integrations in the future, we will notify visitors and request consent through a cookie banner.

Third-party services

Our site is hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect limited technical information to operate and secure the service. You can view GitHub’s privacy policy here: https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement.

If you choose to contact us via Telegram, please note that Telegram’s platform policies apply: https://telegram.org/privacy.

Data retention

We retain contact information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have rights under data protection law, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at: Emnet@emnetcm.com.
