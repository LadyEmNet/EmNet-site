How we work

EmNet offers two ways of working with us:

  • Individual services — choose only what you need, such as an audit, a bot build, or reporting.
  • AIS package — our full Audit, Implement, Sustain framework delivered end-to-end.

Either path gives you practical operations that are accountable, measurable, and resilient.

AIS

AIS is our full framework: Audit, Implement, Sustain. It starts with a hands-on review, then structured improvements and long-term stability.

Audit > Implement > Sustain

Audit

Purpose: Experience your community as a member would, then map the systems behind it.

What we do

  • Join the group to get a first-hand user experience — onboarding, rules, flow of conversation.
  • Review all active bots, commands, and functions in place.
  • Check how data is being captured, if at all (logs, Sheets, reporting hooks).
  • Identify risks in the current bot stack — permissions, admin exposure, gaps in coverage.
  • Assess security weak points and common failure points.
  • Review any use of social growth platforms (airdrops, invite trackers) and educate owners on the risks they pose.

What you get

  • A concise audit report outlining current setup.
  • A list of existing tools and their functions.
  • Risks flagged with explanations in plain English.
  • Quick wins that can be implemented immediately with the current bot stack.
  • Education on the pitfalls of artificial growth platforms.

Implement

Purpose: Deploy processes, automation, and playbooks that stabilise your community.

What we do

  • Define clear roles, coverage schedules, and escalation paths.
  • Configure or build bots: onboarding, quizzes, XP systems, spam filters, and on-chain checks.
  • Establish baseline metrics: retention, response time, solved issues, contributor growth.
  • Pilot in a smaller group if needed, then roll out.
  • Train moderators on rules, tools, and incident handling.
  • Launch with clear communication to the community.

What you get

  • A working playbook and rota.
  • Configured bots with documentation.
  • Training materials and moderator runbooks.
  • Baseline data for future reports.

Sustain

Purpose: Keep systems running smoothly and evolving with the community.

What we do

  • Monitor channels and dashboards for risks and anomalies.
  • Tune bot thresholds and rules as behaviour changes.
  • Provide monthly reports with retention, solved issues, and engagement depth.
  • Hold review sessions to agree changes and priorities.
  • Run incident reviews and update playbooks after events.
  • Introduce sustainable gamified features — e.g. a custom quiz function tailored to your company, automated for ongoing engagement.
  • Suggest improvements to keep features fresh while avoiding hollow “growth hacks.”

What you get

  • Ongoing stability and reduced incidents.
  • Monthly reporting that separates signal from noise.
  • Automated gamified features that engage without draining resources.
  • A living system that adapts as your project and community grow.

Individual services

  • Custom bot builds — built to order for your needs
  • Bluesky analytics reports — send a username and timeframe for a clear engagement report
  • Standard moderation — reliable coverage at a reasonable rate
